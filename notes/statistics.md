# Statistics

## Mean

$$
\bar{x} = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i = 1}^{n}{x_{i}}
$$

## Median

$$
\tilde{x} =
    \begin{cases}
        x_{\frac{n + 1}{2}} & \text{ if odd} \\
        \frac{1}{2}\left(x_{\frac{n}{2}} + x_{\frac{n}{2} + 1}\right) & \text{ if even}
    \end{cases}
$$
